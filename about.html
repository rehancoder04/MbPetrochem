About Us

Maa Bijasani Petro Chem Pvt. Ltd. Is a trusted name in the chemical manufacturing sector, specializing in the production and supply of high-quality Petroleum Ether and Industrial Solvents. Based on a strong foundation of technical expertise and industry experience, our company is committed to delivering reliable, consistent and high-performance chemical solutions to clients across diverse industrial segments. Since our inception, we have focused on maintaining Strick quality control, operational efficiency and customer-centric service, which has helped us build a strong reputation in the market.

Our manufacturing facility is equipped with modern infrastructure and advanced machinery, allowing us to meet bulk requirements without compromising on quality or timelines. Each batch undergoes thorough testing to ensure it meets industry standards for purity, composition and performance. Our products are widely used in laboratories, pharmaceuticals, paint, manufacturing, Cosmetics, adhesives and various other industrial applications.

Furthermore, our company believes in fostering long-term business relationships by providing prompt deliveries, customized solutions and transparent dealings. Our goal is to continuously evolve with industry advancements while upholding our commitment to quality, safety and environmental responsibility.

OUR PRODUCTS' USES

  • 1. Pharmaceuticals: Used as solvents or in formulations for medicinal products.
  • 2. Fine Chemicals: Applied in chemical synthesis and as a solvent in manufacturing specialty chemicals.
  • 4. Paint Industries: Used as a thinner and solvent in paint formulations.
  • 3. Rubber Industries: Used in rubber formulations and processing.
  • 5. Cosmetic: Ether used primarily as solvent
  • 6. Adhesives Industries: Applied as a solvent for adhesives and bonding agents.
