About Us

We are leading manufacturer of Specialty Chemicals and Industrial Solvents in the State of Maharashtra. The Company was founded in 1998 with name Maa Bijasani Petro Chem Private Limited at Dhule District of Maharashtra State with intention to serve industrial customers with vide range of specialty products.

We are master in manufacturing best quality petroleum ether in ten different grades and industrial solvent with our state of art manufacturing facility. We are providing the products to leading brands in Pharmaceuticals, Fine Chemicals, Paint Industries, Rubber Industries, Cosmetic, Adhesives Industries Veterinary Industries, etc. in India.

We believe in Delivering Quality products to our valued customers and we never compromise on quality standards. With the purpose of continuous innovations in the products Maa Bijasani Petro Chem Pvt Ltd works with customers to deliver innovative products and solutions while maintaining a commitment to Safety and Sustainability and Quality. The company’s innovation-driven growth model takes advantage of world-class technology platforms, deep customer engagement, and differentiated application development to grow its leading positions in the markets.

Since its establishment, the company has experienced a rapid growth, thus, becoming one of the leading petroleum ether and industrial solvent Manufacturer in Maharashtra. To achieve Quality excellence in food products the company has established State of the art automatic Manufacturing Plant at Dhule in 1998.

OUR PRODUCTS' USES

  • 1. Pharmaceuticals: Used as solvents or in formulations for medicinal products.
  • 2. Fine Chemicals: Applied in chemical synthesis and as a solvent in manufacturing specialty chemicals.
  • 4. Paint Industries: Used as a thinner and solvent in paint formulations.
  • 3. Rubber Industries: Used in rubber formulations and processing.
  • 5. Cosmetic: Ether used primarily as solvent
  • 6. Adhesives Industries: Applied as a solvent for adhesives and bonding agents.
  • 7. Veterinary Industries: Used as solvents or in formulations for medicinal products
